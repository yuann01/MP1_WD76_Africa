Burnam Park

Burnam Park

Located at the heart of Baguio City, Burnham Park is a picturesque urban park offering various recreational activities. Visitors can enjoy boating in the man-made lake, rent bicycles, have picnics, or simply take a leisurely stroll amidst beautiful gardens and tall pine trees.

The Mansion

The Mansion

This grand presidential mansion is the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. The Mansion showcases elegant architecture, manicured gardens, and a magnificent fa√ßade. Although visitors cannot enter the building itself, they can take pictures in front of it and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Mines View Park

Mines View Park

Situated on a hilltop, Mines View Park offers breathtaking panoramic views of Baguio's mining town and the Cordillera Mountains. Visitors can have their photos taken in traditional Igorot attire, shop for souvenirs at the local market, and enjoy snacks while savoring the picturesque landscapes.

Session Road

Session Road

Known as the main commercial street in Baguio, Session Road offers a vibrant atmosphere with numerous shops, restaurants, and cafes. Visitors can explore the local boutiques, indulge in delicious street food, or simply take a stroll and immerse themselves in the city's bustling vibe.

Botanical Garden

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio showcases a variety of plants, flowers, and trees, both indigenous and exotic. It features charming walkways, stone steps, and ethnic huts representing the different cultural groups in the region. Visitors can take a leisurely walk, learn about the local flora, and appreciate the natural beauty.

Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay

Step into history at Camp John Hay, a former rest and recreation facility for the United States Armed Forces. Today, it's a charming resort complex that features a golf course, luxurious accommodations, and a variety of recreational activities. Don't miss the Bell House and Amphitheater for an enriching experience.

The Mansion

The Mansion

Horseback riding enthusiasts will delight in Wright Park's famous horse rides. You can also witness skilled horseback riders dressed in traditional Igorot attire at the Riding Circle. It's a unique and delightful sight to behold.

Tam-Awan Village

Tam-Awan Village

Experience the indigenous culture of the Cordillera tribes at Tam-Awan Village. This reconstructed traditional village offers a glimpse into the customs, arts, and crafts of the region's native communities. Engage in workshops and demonstrations or simply revel in the serene surroundings.

BenCab Museum

BenCab Museum

Art enthusiasts will find solace in the BenCab Museum. Nestled in the mountains, this museum showcases the works of national artist Benedicto Cabrera (BenCab) and various contemporary Filipino artists. The stunning view of the surrounding forest is an added bonus.

Strawberry Farm

Strawberry Farm

Head to nearby La Trinidad, Benguet, to experience strawberry picking at the Strawberry Farm. Enjoy the cool weather as you harvest fresh strawberries and savor other strawberry-based products like jams and desserts.
