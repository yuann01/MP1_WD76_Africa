Top 5 Baguio Spots


Burnam Park

Located at the heart of Baguio City, Burnham Park is a picturesque urban park offering various recreational activities. Visitors can enjoy boating in the man-made lake, rent bicycles, have picnics, or simply take a leisurely stroll amidst beautiful gardens and tall pine trees.

Learn More
The Mansion

This grand presidential mansion is the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. The Mansion showcases elegant architecture, manicured gardens, and a magnificent façade. Although visitors cannot enter the building itself, they can take pictures in front of it and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Learn More
Session Road

Situated on a hilltop, Mines View Park offers breathtaking panoramic views of Baguio's mining town and the Cordillera Mountains. Visitors can have their photos taken in traditional Igorot attire, shop for souvenirs at the local market, and enjoy snacks while savoring the picturesque landscapes.

Learn More
Botanical Garden

Known as the main commercial street in Baguio, Session Road offers a vibrant atmosphere with numerous shops, restaurants, and cafes. Visitors can explore the local boutiques, indulge in delicious street food, or simply take a stroll and immerse themselves in the city's bustling vibe.

Learn More
Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio showcases a variety of plants, flowers, and trees, both indigenous and exotic. It features charming walkways, stone steps, and ethnic huts representing the different cultural groups in the region. Visitors can take a leisurely walk, learn about the local flora, and appreciate the natural beauty.
